## Research Experience

**AutoBioML: Self-Evolving Research Agent System** *(Nov 2025 – Present)*  
Supervisor: Liu Dianbo · Leader

Developed a multi-agent system (AutoBioML) to automate end-to-end biomedical machine learning workflows using LLM-based orchestration. Constructed a modular framework of 8 specialized sub-agents (Literature Search, Experiment Designer, Coder, etc.) with three bounded feedback loops driven by Critic Agents. Integrated a comprehensive memory architecture (Episodic, Semantic, Procedural) based on the RISE framework, enabling the system to self-evolve by crystallizing successful patterns into persistent storage.

---

**Deep Learning for Proteomics Prediction** *(Jul 2025 – Nov 2025)*  
Supervisor: Shao Wenguang · Leader

Developed a deep learning model for proteomics spectrum prediction. Processed raw spectra using MaxQuant, Comet, and X!Tandem. Built and trained a Transformer on a large-scale spectral dataset using PyTorch, with a dual-head decoder to predict ion distributions hierarchically (b/y-ions → m-ions).

---

**Metabolomic Analysis for Neurodegenerative Disease** *(Oct 2023 – Jun 2025)*  
Supervisor: Ran Jinjun · Shanghai Municipal Undergraduate Innovation Program

Identified plasma triglycerides (TG) and the atherogenic index of plasma (AIP) as metabolic markers through their significant inverse associations with MRI-derived brain grey matter volumes, contributing to early-stage indicators for Alzheimer's Disease.

---

**Molecular Biology of DNA Replication** *(Jan – Sept 2023)*  
Supervisor: Meng Xiangzhou · Intern

Investigated the biological significance of the N-terminal connection between MCM-2/MCM-3 and Pol III in yeast DNA replication using CRISPR and PCR techniques.
